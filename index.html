About

Clarity, Fit, and Confidentiality

Willow Crest Partners is a buy-side advisory firm that works exclusively with established veterinary and pet services companies. Transitioning a practice you've built is deeply personal, and our role is to make discreet, well-matched introductions — not run a broad public process.

Why Practice Owners Choose Us

  • Veterinary sector expertise. We understand the nuances of practice operations, patient care standards, and staff culture.
  • Efficient path. We introduce only well-fitted investors who value quality animal care and team continuity.
  • Owner-first guidance. We support preparation while protecting client relationships and practice goodwill.

What "Buy-Side" Means for Pet Businesses

  • Mission-aligned partners. Investors who share your commitment to animal welfare and clinical excellence.
  • You stay in control. We facilitate introductions; you drive terms and timing.
  • Confidential process. Only curated fits — no broad marketing, no public listings.

Focus

Veterinary & Pet Services We Serve

We work with founder-led, platform-ready veterinary and pet services businesses exhibiting recurring client demand, strong unit economics, and scalable operating models suited for buy-and-build strategies and multi-site expansion.

General Practice Veterinary Clinics

Small animal, mixed, and companion animal practices with loyal client bases, recurring wellness revenue, and strong geographic presence positioned for DSO-style consolidation.

Specialty & Referral Hospitals

Multi-specialty and emergency veterinary hospitals offering cardiology, oncology, surgery, neurology, and critical care — high-value referral networks with defensible competitive positioning.

Emergency & Critical Care Centers

24/7 emergency veterinary facilities and after-hours urgent care clinics with predictable demand, strong referral relationships, and scalable multi-location footprints.

Veterinary Dental Services

Veterinary dental specialty practices and general practices with strong dental programs — a fast-growing consolidation vertical driven by increased pet owner awareness and recurring procedure demand.

Pet Wellness & Preventive Care

Subscription-based wellness plans, preventive care clinics, and low-cost vaccine and wellness platforms generating predictable monthly recurring revenue and high client retention.

Animal Rehabilitation & Physical Therapy

Canine and feline rehabilitation centers, hydrotherapy facilities, and post-surgical recovery clinics with strong specialist referral networks and premium service positioning.

Pet Boarding, Daycare & Grooming

Premium boarding, daycare, and grooming businesses with recurring revenue, branded client loyalty, and strong unit economics aligned with consolidation and franchise expansion strategies.

Veterinary Diagnostic Laboratories

Reference labs, in-house diagnostic services, and imaging centers serving practice networks — a capital-efficient, high-margin consolidation opportunity within the veterinary ecosystem.

Pet Pharmacy & Compounding

Veterinary compounding pharmacies, online pet pharmacies, and prescription fulfillment platforms serving practice networks with recurring prescription demand and growing e-commerce channels.

How We Work

Our Role

Professional Introductions to the Right Partners

We are not brokers or bankers. We connect established veterinary and pet services businesses with vetted investors actively seeking acquisitions, and we keep the process clear and confidential.

1

Initial Conversation

We begin with a private discussion about your goals, concerns, and what matters most to you in a potential partner. This conversation helps us understand your practice culture, staff, client relationships, and long-term vision for the business you've built.

Initial call
2

Profile Preparation

We collaborate with you to organize the essential information investors typically expect — keeping details high-level until you're ready to proceed. This ensures you're well-prepared while protecting client confidentiality and practice goodwill.

Document review
3

Curated Introductions

We make targeted introductions only to strategic fits — vetted investors who align with your goals, values, and commitment to quality animal care. No cold calls, no broad marketing, and no approaches that risk exposing your transition to staff or clients prematurely.

Targeted outreach
4

Direct Engagement

Once connected, you engage directly with potential partners while we step back to allow authentic relationship building. We remain available for guidance and support throughout the process as you evaluate fit.

Your timeline
5

Your Decision, Your Timeline

Whether you choose to move quickly or take time to carefully evaluate your options, the pace and final outcome remain entirely under your control. We support whatever decision serves your best interests and the future of your practice.
